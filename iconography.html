Iconography


Icons are designed to be simple, modern, friendly, and sometimes quirky. Each icon is reduced to its minimal form, expressing essential characteristics.
Material Icons have shapes that are bold and geometric. They have a symmetrical and consistent look, ensuring readability and clarity, even at small sizes.

Click here to go to Google's official Material Icons page. It will provide you with more examples for the icons which are fully compatible with Neolythe.
